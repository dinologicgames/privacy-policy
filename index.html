Users located in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland may be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown. If consent is not granted, non-personalized ads may be displayed instead.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected unintentionally, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Advertising shown to users identified as children is configured to comply with applicable regulations, including COPPA and Google Play Families policies where applicable.
